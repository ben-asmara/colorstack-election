Seven positions are open for the 2025–2026 academic year. Read each description before applying.

Vice President
Leadership · Operations
The VP acts as the president's right hand — stepping in when needed and ensuring board operations run smoothly across all teams.
  • Support the president in all chapter decisions
  • Facilitate and lead board meetings
  • Serve as chapter liaison to national ColorStack
  • Oversee inter-committee collaboration
  • Step in as acting president when unavailable
LeadershipCommunicationOrganization
💰
Treasurer
Finance · Administration
The Treasurer manages all chapter finances — from applying for funding to tracking every dollar spent on events and operations.
  • Manage and track the chapter's annual budget
  • Submit funding requests to the university
  • Process reimbursements and vendor payments
  • Report financial status at board meetings
  • Identify sponsorship and external funding opportunities
BudgetingAttention to DetailSpreadsheets
📋
Secretary
Communications · Records
The Secretary keeps the chapter organized and informed — from documenting decisions to coordinating internal communications across the board.
  • Take and distribute minutes for all board meetings
  • Manage chapter email inbox and correspondence
  • Maintain records, files, and meeting calendars
  • Send reminders and follow-ups to board members
  • Track action items from meetings to completion
WritingOrganizationFollow-through
🎉
Event Coordinator
Events · Programming
The Event Coordinator brings the chapter to life — planning everything from casual hangouts to large career events that make members feel at home.
  • Plan and execute 2–4 events per semester
  • Coordinate venues, catering, and logistics
  • Collaborate with other boards on joint events
  • Manage event RSVPs and day-of coordination
  • Gather and incorporate member feedback
Event PlanningLogisticsCreativity
📣
Marketing & Outreach
Brand · Growth
Marketing & Outreach is the public face of ColorStack — growing our membership, building our brand, and making sure students know they belong here.
  • Manage Instagram, LinkedIn, and other social channels
  • Design flyers, graphics, and promotional materials
  • Lead tabling and recruitment drives
  • Coordinate with clubs and departments for cross-promotion
  • Track engagement and membership growth metrics
Social MediaDesign (Canva/Figma)Storytelling
💻
Tech Lead
Technology · Infrastructure
The Tech Lead ensures our chapter runs on reliable tools and that members have access to technical resources, workshops, and a strong digital presence.
  • Maintain and update the chapter website
  • Manage digital tools (Discord, Notion, Google Drive)
  • Organize and lead technical skill-building workshops
  • Identify and onboard useful technology for the board
  • Support members with technical interview prep resources
Web DevTech ToolsTeaching
🚀
Professional Development
Career · Mentorship
The Professional Development lead connects members to internships, full-time roles, and mentors — making sure every ColorStack member has a clear path forward.
  • Organize resume reviews, mock interviews & workshops
  • Build relationships with company recruiters
  • Coordinate mentorship pairing with alumni/professionals
  • Curate and share job, internship, and scholarship opportunities
  • Plan career fairs and industry networking nights
Career CoachingNetworkingMentorship
